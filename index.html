The Supercars of Dubai

Dubai isn't a city — it's a showroom with traffic lights. Cruise down Sheikh Zayed Road for ten minutes and you'll see more horsepower than most countries register in a year. This page is a quick rundown of the supercars you can actually rent and drive here, because in Dubai the dream isn't owning one, it's booking one for the weekend.

Lamborghini Huracán — the gateway supercar. 5.2L V10, 631 hp, and a soundtrack that will set off every car alarm from JBR to Downtown. Rents for around AED 2,500/day and it's the most-Instagrammed plate in the city for a reason.

Ferrari 488 Spider — drop the top, hit the Palm Jumeirah crescent at sunset, and try not to grin. Twin-turbo V8, 661 hp, and the chassis feels surgical. The classic Dubai larp move.

McLaren 720S — if the Lambo is the loud one, this is the precise one. 710 hp, dihedral doors, and an aero package that looks like it landed from another planet. The car that will actually scare you on the Jebel Jais switchbacks.

Rolls-Royce Cullinan — not a supercar in the traditional sense, but if you're cosplaying as Dubai royalty for a weekend, this is the vehicle. 6.75L twin-turbo V12, infinite leather, and a ride so smooth you'll forget about every speed bump in Business Bay.

Bugatti Chiron — the boss level. 1,479 hp, 261 mph top speed, and a rental price that resembles a small mortgage. You'll see them parked outside the Burj Al Arab as casually as Civics in the rest of the world.

Whether it's a sunrise run to Hatta or a slow crawl past the Burj Khalifa fountains, Dubai is the one city built for the supercar fantasy. Pick a car, pick a road, and live the larp.
